My theme-park story starts in a stroller at Phantasialand's fairy-tale lake,
and the rides through the legendary "1001 Nights" gondola with my dad turned
it into a lifelong love. The other half of me spent 25+ years building
software — leading development at CyberGhost, engineering at ExpressVPN —
systems millions of people rely on without ever noticing them.

park.fan is where the two halves finally met: live data from 150+ theme
parks and 5,000+ attractions, plus an AI that sees crowds coming up to a
year ahead — so time in a park goes to magic, not railings. On the blog I
write trip reports, data deep-dives and everything the numbers alone can't
explain.
