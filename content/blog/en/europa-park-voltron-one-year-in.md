When [Voltron Nevera](attraction:europa-park/voltron-nevera-powered-by-rimac)
opened at [Europa-Park](park:europa-park) in spring 2024, the marketing
called it a "new era". The internet, predictably, called it both the best
coaster in Europe and a glorified family ride. A year of operating data
later, we can settle at least one of those debates with numbers.

## The headliner question

Before Voltron, the de-facto headliner queue at Europa-Park belonged to
[Silver Star](attraction:europa-park/silver-star) — the 2002 B&M hyper
that anchors the French area. Silver Star's typical peak-hour wait,
averaged across the 2023 summer season, was **52 minutes**.

```glossary-widget slug=hyper-coaster
```

In 2025's comparable summer window, two things happened:

1. Voltron's peak-hour wait averaged **74 minutes**. Headliner, confirmed.
2. **Silver Star's** peak-hour wait dropped to **39 minutes** — a 25%
   reduction. Same operations, same crowd, just a new headliner pulling
   pressure off.

<!--tbl-theme: success-->

| Ride          | 2023 peak wait | 2025 peak wait |
| ------------- | -------------- | -------------- |
| Voltron Nevera | —             | 74 min         |
| Silver Star   | 52 min         | 39 min         |
| Wodan         | 41 min         | 48 min         |

The most-loaded ride after Voltron's first full year is still Voltron.
That doesn't surprise anyone. But the **second** most-loaded ride is now
[Wodan – Timburcoaster](attraction:europa-park/wodan-timburcoaster), not
Silver Star. The center of gravity in the park has shifted east-to-north.

## Crowd patterns within the park

The data also shows a clean **morning herd** effect. On a typical Saturday,
the first hour of operating sees 40% of all park entries head directly to
the Croatian area — Voltron's neighbourhood. By **11:30**, Voltron's queue
peaks. By **14:00**, Silver Star's queue peaks. By **17:00**, both have
collapsed and Wodan owns the evening rush.

If you're planning a visit, the practical takeaway is annoying but simple:

- **Do Voltron first thing** or it dominates your day.
- **Do Silver Star late afternoon** — the line is half what it was at noon.
- **Wodan is the evening ride.** Its line drops faster than Voltron's after
  19:00 in summer.

## Is it the best coaster in Europe?

Personal opinion, irrelevant to the data, take it or leave it: it's in the
top three, but it's not number one. The launches are genuinely excellent.
The mid-course transitions feel slightly underpowered. The theming and the
ride-experience-as-a-package _is_ the best in Europe, full stop.

For the data answer to "did it change Europa-Park" — yes. Permanently and
measurably. The park is busier on Voltron's side, calmer on Silver Star's
side, and the overall capacity story is healthier than it was in 2023.
